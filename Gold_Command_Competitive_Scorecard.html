🚀 FREE-TIER IMPROVEMENTS TO CLOSE THE GAP

These improvements use only free APIs, open data, and existing infrastructure — no paid subscriptions needed.

HIGH IMPACT (Could add +8–12 points to composite score)

1. Add COT Positioning Data
HIGH IMPACT · FREE
The biggest missing feature. CFTC publishes COT data every Friday as free public domain data. The cot_reports Python library can pull gold futures positioning (net long/short, changes, managed money vs commercials) directly.

Implementation: Weekly fetch of CFTC disaggregated report → extract gold futures (code 088691) → show net speculative position, weekly change, and historical chart.

Why it matters: Pro traders consider COT data essential. Your Pro view currently says "Monitor COT report" but doesn't show the data. This closes that gap entirely — for free.
Source: cot-reports.com · CFTC.gov · github.com/NDelventhal/cot_reports
2. Add News Sentiment Scoring
HIGH IMPACT · FREE
Currently news is categorised but not sentiment-scored. Use Alpha Vantage's free News & Sentiment API (25 calls/day free) to add AI-powered sentiment scores to gold-related headlines.

Implementation: Fetch Alpha Vantage news sentiment for "gold" topic → merge scores with existing RSS feed → show aggregate bullish/bearish sentiment gauge.

Why it matters: Transforms the news tab from "here's what happened" to "here's what the market thinks about what happened." FXStreet Premium charges for this level of analysis.
Source: alphavantage.co/documentation/#news-sentiment
3. Add Intraday Data via Finnhub
HIGH IMPACT · FREE (60 calls/min)
The biggest gap vs TradingView is the 15-minute data delay and daily-only candles. Finnhub's free tier provides real-time forex quotes and 1-minute candle data for XAU/USD.

Implementation: Use Finnhub WebSocket or REST for live XAU/USD quotes → replace yfinance for spot price → add 1H/4H intraday views.

Why it matters: Moves data freshness from "10–15 min delay" to "near real-time" — the single biggest reliability gap.
Source: finnhub.io/docs/api · 60 API calls/min free tier
4. Session-Based Range Breakdown
HIGH IMPACT · FREE
You have the session clock but not session-specific ranges. With intraday data (from Finnhub), compute Asian range, London range, NY range for today — and historical averages.

Implementation: Segment intraday candles by session UTC windows → compute OHLC per session → compare to 20-day session averages.

Why it matters: Professional gold traders plan entries around session ranges. "Gold moved 60% of its ADR during Asia" is an actionable insight that no competitor surfaces automatically.
Uses: Finnhub intraday data + existing session window definitions

MEDIUM IMPACT (Could add +4–6 points)

5. Gold ETF Flow Tracker
MEDIUM IMPACT · FREE
Track GLD and IAU ETF volumes as a proxy for institutional gold flows. yfinance already provides this data — just add GLD/IAU volume to the dashboard with a "flow indicator" (volume vs 20-day average).

Why it matters: ETF flows are a leading indicator. Heavy GLD buying = institutional conviction. This is data Bloomberg terminal users pay $2,000/mo for, available free via yfinance.
6. Gold/Silver Ratio Monitor
MEDIUM IMPACT · FREE
The gold/silver ratio is a key intermarket signal. You already fetch both instruments — just compute GC=F / SI=F and show current ratio vs 20/50/200-day averages with interpretation (e.g., "Ratio at 88 = historically elevated → silver may outperform").

Why it matters: Precious metals traders watch this religiously. Free to compute, high perceived value.
7. Fear & Greed Composite Index
MEDIUM IMPACT · FREE
Build a gold-specific fear & greed index from data you already have: VIX level, RSI, price vs SMA, volume ratio, DXY direction, yield direction. Composite score 0–100 with "Extreme Fear / Fear / Neutral / Greed / Extreme Greed" labels.

Why it matters: CNN has one for stocks. Nobody has one specifically for gold. This becomes a signature feature and a shareable metric for Telegram/social media.
8. Historical Pattern Recognition
MEDIUM IMPACT · FREE
Use 6 months of daily data to detect basic candlestick patterns (engulfing, doji, hammer, shooting star) programmatically with TA-Lib or custom logic. Display the last 3 confirmed patterns with historical win rates.

Why it matters: Your signal engine already detects some patterns, but surfacing them with historical context adds credibility. Free to compute.
